About OpenRiskOps

OpenRiskOps is an infrastructure-agnostic, open-source platform that integrates leading tools like OpenRMF, TheHive, Cortex, and OpenCTI to centralize risk management, incidents, threats, and corrective actions. Perfect for enterprises seeking seamless compliance and threat intelligence.

Key Features

Risk Registry

Manage compliance and risks with OpenRMF integration.

Incident Management

Handle investigations with TheHive.

Automation

Execute analyses with Cortex playbooks.

Threat Intelligence

Contextualize threats with OpenCTI.
